Katye

Are you as frustrated as us with the world politics situation? Maybe you want to see some change happening in the near future, aka 2020?

Katye is your perfect solution! This simple chrome extension replaces all text instances of Donald Trump with "kanye2020" and all images with kittens thereby creating a more pleasant internet experience for any chrome user.


EXAMPLE

I love Donald Trump

Trump is so great

Donald John Trump is my hero


About Katye

Built in a 24-hour hackathon at Concordia University, Katye is a fun Google Chrome extension built to replace all online images with cats and replace any mentions of Donald Trump with kanye2020
